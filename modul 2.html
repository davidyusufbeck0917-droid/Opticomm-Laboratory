Module 2

Modul 2

Focuses on optical fiber network performance measurement techniques using instruments such as OTDR and OPM. Students learn how OTDR is used to detect faults, measure fiber length, and determine attenuation along the transmission path. In addition, the Optical Power Meter (OPM) is used to measure optical power levels. Through hands-on practice and data analysis, students are trained to interpret measurement results for evaluating network quality and troubleshooting purposes.
