Module 1

Modul 1

Discusses the fundamental concepts of optical fiber and the measurement of Numerical Aperture (NA). This module explains the structure and working principles of optical fiber, including how light propagates through the fiber core. It also introduces the concept of NA as an important parameter that determines the light acceptance capability of the fiber. Students perform practical measurements of NA to understand the basic characteristics of optical fiber directly. The purpose of this module is to build a strong theoretical foundation before moving to measurement and installation practices.
